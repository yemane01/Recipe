A Reciepe for T-bone Steak

Here is a simple step by step instructions by tipbuzz.

Instructions

  1. Let steak sit at room temperature for 30-60 minutes before cooking.
  2. Pat dry the steaks with paper towels.
  3. Rub the steak with oil, and then season with salt and pepper on both sides.
  4. Heat the pan over medium-high heat. Add the steaks and cook until browned on
  5. Heat a cast-iron skillet over high heat.
  6. Add oil to the hot skillet and add steak when it begins to smoke.
  7. Sear the steak for 2 minutes for the first side. Flip and sear 2 minutes.
  8. Add minced garlic, butter and fresh rosemary.
  9. Transfer the steak to a preheated oven at 425°F (218°C).
  10. Cook until it reaches the desired doneness.
  11. Transfer steak to a plate or cutting board and let it rest for 5 minutes.
  12. Cut steak from the bone to serve.

Ingredients

1 ½ pounds: Bone Steak (about 1-inch thick)

2 tablespoons: Olive Oil

1 teaspoon Kosher: Salt (or to taste)

1 teaspoon: Cracked Black Pepper

1 teaspoon: Garlic (minced)

1 tablespoon: Butter

1 tablespoon: Fresh Rosemary
